How to Update a Page in GitLab

  1. Open your project in GitLab.
  2. Click the file you want to edit (like index.html).
  3. Click the pencil icon (✏️) to edit.
  4. Make your changes in the editor.
  5. Scroll down to the Commit changes section.
  6. Write a short commit message (example: “Updated homepage”).
  7. Select Commit to main.
  8. Click Commit changes.
  9. Wait a few seconds, then refresh your live site.

Your site will rebuild automatically after each commit.
